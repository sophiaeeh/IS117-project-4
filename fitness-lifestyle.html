Fitness & Lifestyle

Staying active and taking care of my body is a big part of how I handle stress from school and content creation. On this page, I share realistic ways I fit movement and healthy habits into a busy schedule.

My Weekly Movement Routine

I do not always have time for long workouts, so I focus on consistency over perfection. Some days that means a full gym session, and other days it is just a walk or stretch.

  • Short strength or cardio sessions on class days.
  • Longer workouts or classes on lighter school days.
  • Walking, stretching, or gentle movement when I am low on energy.

Small Lifestyle Habits

I try to make small choices that support my energy: drinking water, getting enough sleep when I can, packing snacks, and setting boundaries around my phone at night.

Simple Habits I Focus On

  • Carrying a water bottle throughout the day
  • Planning one or two movement breaks
  • Putting my phone away before bed when possible

Mindset & Self-Compassion

Balancing everything means accepting that some days will be off. Instead of quitting when I fall behind, I try to reset, adjust my plan, and move forward without guilt.
