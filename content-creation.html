Content Creation

This is where I share how I plan, film, and edit my content while still getting my school work done and taking care of myself.

Planning Posts Around My Schedule

Instead of posting randomly, I create a simple content plan based on my class schedule. I choose days when I know I have more energy or lighter workloads to film and batch content.

Filming & Editing Workflow

I try to film multiple clips in one session so I can edit later in small pockets of time. Short editing sessions between classes or at the end of the day make the process more manageable.

  • Brainstorm ideas and write quick notes in my phone.
  • Batch-film content when I have good lighting and time.
  • Edit on my phone or laptop using small time blocks.

Tools I Use

Some of the tools that make creating easier:

  • Notes app for ideas and scripts
  • Simple editing app for short-form videos
  • Calendar reminders for posting days

Staying Authentic Online

My goal is to show a realistic version of balancing school, fitness, and content, not perfection. Some days are smooth, others are messy, and that is okay. I want people to feel less alone in trying to do it all.
